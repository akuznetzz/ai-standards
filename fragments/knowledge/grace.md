## GRACE Knowledge Structuring
- Use GRACE to structure codebase knowledge before large changes and during handoffs.
- Maintain the following knowledge lenses:
  - Goals: product and task intent.
  - Rules: architectural, business, and operational constraints.
  - Architecture: components, boundaries, interfaces, and ownership.
  - Code: concrete files, modules, and implementation entry points.
  - Evolution: decisions, tradeoffs, and pending follow-ups.
- When a task requires deeper project understanding, update GRACE artifacts or ConPort entries instead of leaving the knowledge implicit.
- Prefer linking decisions and implementation notes so future agents can reconstruct why the current shape exists.
